Basic stew

Ingredients

  • 2 unions
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • few cloves of garlic
  • Nail size of red pepper
  • Meat (Beef, pork, lam, chicken). Can be cheaper but flavorful cuts with bones.
  • 2 tins of tomatoes
  • Red wine (or beer)
  • Herbs (bay leaf, rosemary, thyme, dry oregano, basil)
  • 2 stock cubes (chicken, beef or vegetable)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop your vegetables fine and cut your meat in inch size cubes.
  2. Slowly fry your soffritto in some olive oil until soft.
  3. Add the meat and fry more to give the meat color on the outside.
  4. Add a two glases of red wine to stop the frying. And take a sip yourself.
  5. Cook off the wine and add the two tins of tomatoes.
  6. Stirr and break up the tomatoes with your wooden spoon.
  7. Fill both tins with water to clean them and add both. Don't waist anything.
  8. Add the stock cubes and herbs.
  9. Turn the fire as low a possible and simmer for a few hours.
  10. Enjoy your bottle of wine.
  11. Stirr a bit to losen up the sticky bits at the bottom and sides of the pan. Pure flavour.
  12. After a few hours the meat should be soft and tender. You can easily break up the meat with your fingers.
  13. Remove the herbs and add some salt and peper to adjust the flavour.
