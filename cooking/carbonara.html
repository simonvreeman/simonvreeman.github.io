Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Guanciale or Pancetta
  • Organic eggs
  • Parmigiano Reggiano or Pecorino Romano
  • Spaghettoni
  • Pepper and salt

Instructions

  1. Put a large pan for cooking the spaghettoni on the stove with a good amount of water plus a good pinch of salt.
  2. Cut the guanciale in small pieces.
  3. Bake the guanciale in a pan. No need to add oil.
  4. Cook the spaghettoni until al dente.
  5. Meanwhile break some eggs in a bowl. Put an extra egg yolk in it to make it taste richer. For two person I use 2 eggs plus an extra yolk.
  6. Add a good handfull of grated cheese, a pinch of pepper and mix it together.
  7. Once the spaghettoni is al dente, turn of the heat of both pans. Add the spaghettoni to the quanciale and mix it together.
  8. Add the cheese and egg mix and mix it through the spaghettoni and guanciale. The pan should not be too hot, otherwise the egg will cook. That's what you don't want.
  9. If needed, add a few tables spoons of cooking water to losing up the sauce.
  10. Finish off with some freshly grounded black pepper.
