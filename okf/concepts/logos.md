# Logos

In Stoicism, the all-pervading rational principle that orders and directs the universe—synonymous with nature, Providence, or God—and present in each person as the faculty of reason.

## Related

- Part of [Stoicism](./stoicism.md).
- Described by [Meditations](../works/meditations.md).

## Examples

> Logos operates both in individuals and in the universe as a whole. In individuals it is the faculty of reason. On a cosmic level it is the rational principle that governs the organization of the universe. [1]
