# The four cardinal virtues

The Stoic virtues Marcus highlights (Meditations 3.6): wisdom, justice, courage, and self-control (temperance), held as the highest goods.

## Related

- Part of [Stoicism](./stoicism.md).
- Described by [Meditations](../works/meditations.md).

## Examples

> If, at some point in your life, you should come across anything better than justice, honesty, self-control, courage—it must be an extraordinary thing indeed—and enjoy it to the full. [1]
