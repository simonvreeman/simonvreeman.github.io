# Memento mori

Remembrance of death as a guide to action, as in Marcus's 'You could leave life right now—let that determine what you do and say and think' (Meditations 2.11).

## Related

- Part of [Stoicism](./stoicism.md).
- Described by [Meditations](../works/meditations.md).

## Examples

> You could leave life right now. Let that determine what you do and say and think. [1]
