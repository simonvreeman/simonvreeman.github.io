# On the Shortness of Life

Seneca's essay De Brevitate Vitae, arguing that life is not short but largely wasted, and urging it be lived wisely.

## Related

- Written by [Seneca the Younger](../persons/seneca.md).
- An instance of [Stoicism](../concepts/stoicism.md).

## Examples

> It is not that we have a short space of time, but that we waste much of it. Life is long enough, and it has been given in sufficiently generous measure to allow the accomplishment of the very greatest things if the whole of it is well invested. [1]
