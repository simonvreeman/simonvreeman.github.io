# Fragments of Epictetus

Brief quotations of Epictetus not found in the surviving Discourses, mostly preserved in the anthology of Stobaeus.

## Related

- Written by [Epictetus](../persons/epictetus.md).
- Part of [Discourses of Epictetus](./discourses.md).
- Translated by [Robert Dobbin](../persons/robert-dobbin.md).

## Examples

> Epictetus said that we must find a method for managing assent. In the field of assent we have to be careful to use it with reservation, with restraint and in the service of society. Drop desire altogether and apply aversion to nothing that is not under our control. [1]
