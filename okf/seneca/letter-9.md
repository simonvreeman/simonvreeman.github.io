## Examples

> You desire to know whether Epicurus is right when, in one of his letters,1 he rebukes those who hold that the wise man is selfsufficient and for that reason does not stand in need of friendships. This is the objection raised by Epicurus against Stilbo and those who believe2 that the Supreme Good is a soul which is insensible to feeling. [1]
