Letter 59 of Seneca's Moral Letters to Lucilius: "On Pleasure and Joy".

## Examples

> I received great pleasure from your letter; kindly allow me to use these words in their everyday meaning, without insisting upon their Stoic import. For we Stoics hold that pleasure is a vice. Very likely it is a vice; but we are accustomed to use the word when we wish to indicate a happy state of mind. [1]
