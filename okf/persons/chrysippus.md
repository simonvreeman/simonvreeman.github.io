# Chrysippus of Soli

Third head of the Stoa (c. 279-206 BC), the systematizer who divided philosophy into logic, physics, and ethics and refined the doctrine of pneuma.

## Related

- Related to [Stoicism](../concepts/stoicism.md).

## Examples

> Chrysippus and his followers had divided knowledge into three areas: logic, physics and ethics, concerned, respectively, with the nature of knowledge, the structure of the physical world and the proper role of human beings in that world. [1]
