CRO Tools

An unsorted and living list of Conversion Rate Optimization tools that a CRO specialist needs in their toolbox. New CRO tools are added regularly.

Some of the CRO tools are transparent about their efforts to comply with GDPR. For example Convert and Hotjar. I have added links to those specific GDPR pages for those CRO tools.
