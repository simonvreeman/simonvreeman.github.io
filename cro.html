What are CRO tools?

CRO tools are the tools a CRO specialist uses to make the website and/or app that he/she is optimizing better. These CRO tools will give the CRO specialist the data, insights, help find pain points encountered by users and anwsers to make well-founded decisions. It can range from tools to do experiments with or gather qualitative data by doing surveys, or even monitor the site speed performance of the website.
