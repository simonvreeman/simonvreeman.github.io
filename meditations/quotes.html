# Quoted in The Obstacle Is the Way ↑

The Obstacle Is the Way: The Timeless Art of Turning Trials into Triumph is the first book by Ryan Holiday about modern Stoicism. The book the part of the trilogy which also contains Ego is the Enemy and Stillness is the Key. It teaches you how to let go of the things you can't control and turn every new obstacle into an opportunity to get better, stronger, and tougher.
